Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Tamil Information Centre (TIC) Archives is committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors, researchers, donors, members, and contributors to our digital archive and website. We collect and process personal information responsibly, in line with our mission to preserve Tamil-speaking communities' heritage, history, and human rights documentation.

1. Information We Collect
  • Voluntarily provided data: Name, email address, phone (if provided via contact forms, membership applications, donation, or newsletter subscription)
  • Automatically collected: IP address, browser type/version, device information, pages visited, time/date of access, referral source
  • Archives & Research: When you submit materials (testimonies, documents, photos), we may collect metadata and contact details for verification/authenticity purposes only
2. How We Use Your Information
  • To respond to enquiries, process membership/donations, and send occasional updates/newsletters (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To improve website usability, accessibility, and archival cataloguing
  • For internal research, statistical analysis, and preservation of cultural memory (data is anonymised where possible)
  • We never sell, rent, or trade personal data with third parties for marketing purposes
3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share data only when legally required (court order, national security, etc.) or with trusted service providers (hosting, email service, analytics — all under strict data protection agreements).

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent (where processing is consent-based)
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (if applicable in your jurisdiction)
6. International Transfers

As a global digital archive, data may be transferred to and stored outside your country (including servers in secure jurisdictions). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyse site usage, and ensure site functionality.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences.

2. Cookies We Use
  • Essential / Strictly necessary: Required for basic site functions (navigation, security, form submission)
  • Analytics: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site (e.g. Google Analytics or similar privacy-friendly tools — anonymised)
  • Preferences: Remember language choice, dark mode, or search filters (if implemented)
3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some site features. Most browsers allow you to block third-party cookies.
